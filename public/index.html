The Floor Rules

Keep the hall friendly for everyone. Break these and you can be muted or removed.

  1. 🤝
    Be respectful. No harassment, hate speech, or slurs — in text or voice.
  2. 🧼
    Keep it clean. No explicit, NSFW, or illegal content anywhere on the floor.
  3. 💎
    Trade honestly. Represent your cards accurately. No scams, baits, or fake listings.
  4. 🎙️
    Voice is public. Anyone standing near you can hear you. Mute yourself when you're not talking.
  5. 🚫
    No spam. Don't flood chat or blast the same promo over and over.
  6. 🚩
    Report trouble. Flag bad actors — moderators can mute or remove them.
